Our Story

Born From Love,
Refined by Fire

It started with a grandmother's kitchen in Jaipur, where saffron and ghee were never measured — only felt. When Chef Vikram Anand brought those recipes to Ahmedabad in 2012, he wasn't just opening a restaurant. He was carrying a legacy.

Over the years, Saffron & Ember has grown from a 12-seat tiffin room into one of Gujarat's most celebrated dining destinations — yet the philosophy hasn't changed: cook with soul, serve with warmth.

We source from the same farmers we've partnered with since day one. We still make our own spice blends every Monday morning. And we still believe that the best meal you'll ever have should feel like coming home.

📞 Contact Us
What are your delivery timings and zones? +
We deliver from 11:00 AM to 10:30 PM, seven days a week. We currently cover Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, and Vadodara. Delivery is free on orders above ₹499. For bulk or corporate orders outside our standard zone, please call us directly — we'll always try to accommodate you.
Can I customise dishes for dietary preferences? +
Absolutely. Most of our dishes can be adapted — we offer vegan, gluten-friendly, and Jain-friendly versions. Simply add a note when ordering or call our kitchen line. We handle allergies with utmost care. We do recommend calling ahead for severe allergen concerns so our chef can prepare accordingly.
How do I book a table for a special occasion? +
Use the "Book a Table" form on this page or call +91 98765 43210 to reserve. For special occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, or private dining, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance. We offer customised setups, curated menus, and cake arrangements — just let us know what you have in mind.
Where do your ingredients come from? +
We partner exclusively with local farms within 50 km of our kitchen. Produce is sourced fresh every morning — no frozen shortcuts, no pre-packaged shortcuts. Our spices are hand-blended weekly in-house from whole ingredients. We audit our supply chain quarterly for freshness and sustainability standards.
Do you offer catering for events and corporate lunches? +
Yes — catering is one of our most popular services. From intimate gatherings of 10 to large corporate events of 500+, we provide full-service catering with custom menus, live counters, and on-site staff if required. Please contact us at least 5 days in advance for large orders.
What hygiene and safety standards do you follow? +
Our kitchen is FSSAI-certified and undergoes monthly independent hygiene audits. All kitchen staff are food safety trained, gloves and hair-nets are mandatory, and we use food-grade, eco-friendly packaging. We maintain a 100% no-outside-food-mixing policy to ensure every dish is pure and uncontaminated.
What if my order is late or incorrect? +
We have a simple promise: if your delivery is more than 10 minutes late (beyond our quoted time) or if there's an error, your next order is on us — no questions asked. Call our support line at +91 98765 43210 within 30 minutes of receiving your order and we'll make it right immediately.
